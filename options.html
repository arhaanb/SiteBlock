SiteBlock

SiteBlock is a simple way to block access to websites.

To block a website, type one in the input below and hit enter. For example, facebook.com. To remove a website, just click the cross beside it. Subdomains are not blocked for a root URL. To block all subdomains, enter a site like *.facebook.com. Hit CTRL + K to focus input.
